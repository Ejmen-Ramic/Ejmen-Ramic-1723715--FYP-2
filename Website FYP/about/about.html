About us

LifeLine is a nonprofit that supports other nonprofits by connecting them to donors and companies. Since 2021, we've helped trusted, community-led organizations from Afghanistan to Zimbabwe (and hundreds of places in between) access the tools, training, and support they need to make our world a better place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enable people around the world to help end poverty and fund projects by donating to people in need in a manner where money is not the main source

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the main platform which creates a path where anyone around the world is able to help anyone in need which will enable our mission of ending poverty in this world.
